DGM 2190 Game Languages 1
3 Credits
Fall, Winter

Prerequisites:
DGM 1090

Description:
Teaches basic scripting for games. Topics in this course include: understanding the writing process; writing effective sentences, paragraphs, and short essays; patterns of organization; rhetorical modes and purposes; audience analysis; the composing process; and editing.
